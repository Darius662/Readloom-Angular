About Readloom

Readloom is a digital collection manager that has evolved from its original focus on manga and comics to support a wide variety of media types. It can track manga, comics, text books, light novels, and supports both physical and electronic collections. Readloom follows the design principles of the *arr suite of applications while providing comprehensive collection management capabilities.

This project was created as a fork of the retired Readarr project, focusing specifically on manga and comics while removing some features and adding others to support a broader range of digital media.

Readloom is open source software, and contributions are welcome!
Key Features
  • Interactive release calendar for digital media
  • Track your digital collection (manga, comics, books, light novels)
  • Monitor upcoming releases
  • Support for both physical and electronic collections
  • Home Assistant integration (not yet implemented)
  • Homarr integration (not yet implemented)
  • Modern, responsive web interface
